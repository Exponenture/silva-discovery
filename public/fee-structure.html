The Three Fees
Fee 01
Platform Fee
Discovery description: SaaS fee
R45,000
per month
Flat rate. Same every month from day one, regardless of phase. Covers platform infrastructure, API integration, system maintenance, account management, and monthly portfolio intelligence reports. Paid regardless of customer adoption levels. Subject to annual CPI increases and reviewed based on material portfolio growth.
Fee 02
Digitisation Fee
Discovery description: OCR fee
R22 – R36
per item
Once-off per item. Drops as volume scales. Never charged twice for the same item. R36 at low volumes where templates are built from scratch. Drops to R25 at scale and R22 above 60,000 items. Subject to further discussion at full portfolio scale. New and existing items treated at the same standard.
Fee 03
Always On Fee
Discovery description: VAS monthly fee per item
R3.00
SILVA share per item / month
Activates after digitisation. 60/40 split — Discovery retains the majority. Keeps every insured item valued at current replacement cost. Grows automatically as new clients join. Negligible vs R600 traditional valuation. Subject to annual CPI increases and portfolio size review.

The Bottom Line
Three fees. Three clear deliverables. Platform fee: R45,000 per month flat — one rate, no phases, no adjustments. Digitisation: once-off per item, dropping from R36 to R22 as volume scales. Always On: Discovery retains 60%, SILVA receives 40%. All fees sit within Discovery's R100 per month premium ceiling. All rates are subject to annual CPI increases and reviewed based on material growth in portfolio size. Every rand has a line-item basis approvable independently by Discovery's procurement team.
